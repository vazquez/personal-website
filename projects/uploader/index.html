The 500px Uploader.

View Project
composition

Challenge

At 500px, where I work as a UI/UX Designer, we are constantly seeking to improve our product. Being a product designer means that one has the capacity to nurture and let design breathe into every crevice of the product. I absolutely love immersing myself in a product, making it incrementally better each and every time. There are times, however, where a biggre change is needed. The Upload experience at 500px needed to change for the better in a big way.

Solution

After countless wireframes and a few iterations, the new 500px Uploader was born. This design features the future visual aesthetic of 500px; a glimpse of what's to come. By allowing for a single page experience, we created a more usable interface than our previous implementation. We introduced batch uploads, easy EXIF input, enhanced geolocation, and worked on the UX to make it all make sense. One of the features I am most proud of is Discoverability. Discoverability uses gamification principles to encourage the user to upload the image with as much metadata as possible. Adding your ISO and Shutter Speed has never been so much fun. View the 500px Uploader live at 500px.com/upload.
