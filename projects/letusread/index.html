Let Us Read: UI Artboard

View Project
.png

You know, the key of UI Design is envisioning yourself inside of the experience itself. If one does not imagine, one cannot create. You have to embody. You have to believe. Let Us Read is an Artboard that I created for a reading web app. It features formality,

elegance, and transcendence. This artboard is for the product or experience that wants to cultivate it's readers through the gift of colour and texture. Don't you love being able to feel the Internet? I often find myself touching the screen. Am I alone on this one?

Fun Fact: I will be updating this artboard as I come up with new UI Elements.
