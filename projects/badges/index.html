website

Challenge

Vendor-stakeholder relationships need loving. But I'm not talking about a dinner here and there. What if you could get the stakeholder excited of a project's progress? What, I wonder, would happen then? I know. One would foster a relationship in which change would not be seen as a scary thing; and progress would be welcome like grandma's cookies.

Solution

I, personally, am a sucker for badges. Give me a badge and I'll do just about anything. Let's use badges in the business world. Let's bring excitement to client meetings. For crying out loud, let's have fun. This project showcases badges I have thought of that would be appropriate to use in an Agile or Scrum environment.

Fun Fact: Badges make the world go round.
