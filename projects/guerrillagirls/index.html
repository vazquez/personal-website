Guerrilla Girls Website Redesign

View Project
print

Challenge

Guerrilla Girls, a group of anonymous female artists who take the names of dead women artists as pseudonyms, wanted to reinvent the word 'feminism'. With the aid of a brand new website, they had the opportunity to reach to a larger demographic with a balanced and fiercely feminine design.

Solution

Information is key. Whilst maintaining a maximum amount of informational documents, I re-designed the website to connote an eager, passionate, and powerful website. The website contained custom elements such as header tags (seen top) and jQuery to make the website dynamic while viewing artwork.
