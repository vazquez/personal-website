Hey Darling...

Launch Project
website

Challenge

You know, sometimes it's just so hard to make someone's heart beat right out of their chest. It's something I strive for every day in my work. I love designing emotional experiences, albeit brief, that captivate and engage the user. I constantly look for ways to design for happiness. While brainstorming of 'happy moments', this thought came to mind: What if you were given one photo and one line of text to say everything to a certain someone? Many would find this impossible, some people argue that's all you need. I decided to put this to the test.

Solution

Because my main goal was to immerse the viewer, I decided to go big. I'm using sleek CSS3 properties that allow the photo to expand to every corner of the screen. Then, I used jQuery to help me in making this photographic narrative contain interactive elements. The result is a nostalgic 'photo session'. The image fades-in akin to a flash on a camera. It blinds the user for a split second before revealing a beautiful photo and message. Surprise is in the details. Meaning is in everything we do.

Fun Fact: Photos and text are stored in a JavaScript array. This makes the HTML only 8 lines!
