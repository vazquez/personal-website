Pixel Happiness: A Design Blog.

Launch Project
website

Challenge

Legibility, need I say more? I sought to design a blog that placed legibility above all. No distractions, just you and the words on a screen. It's harder than it sounds, believe me. This is a work in progress, but I love sharing with you the process of an unfinished product. I often find it tells more of a story than the finished product itself. It's all about the journey.

Progress

I am using Middleman, a static site generator, to maintain this code in an elegant and scalable way. I am experimenting against off-white backgrounds to make this virtual reading eperience feel a bit more like home: a real book. I have yet to design a fully reponsive layout, so some parts might be broken. I find that colour is such an important part of this blog. I am experimenting frequently for the perfect colour combination.

Pixelhappiness.com should be live by the summer!
