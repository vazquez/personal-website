Dear Jen: A Responsive Love Letter

Launch Project
website

Challenge

There I was, a week before Valentine's Day. Panic. Okay, so I needed to 'wow' my girlfriend. But how? I wanted to create an effective, but short, digital experience. And, in the process, I was thinking of writing some kick-ass prose. There was one problem, however. I needed to surprise her at 6AM, as she was waking up. What do we do when us young folk wake up? Yes, we check our phones like our life depends on it. I needed to make something that was able to be seen by a computer, a phone, a plasma screen... you name it.

Solution

Bingo! The result was: a Responsive Love Letter. Go ahead and resize the browser. The text will accommodate to any screen resolution, while still maintaining that emotional connection to the copy. By knowing the user, I was able to make a sound judgment to enhance this experience. This was Jen's love letter to be seen by the world. Many people have asked me the same question. Yes, I did write the copy myself. What can I say? I'm a hopeless digital romantic.

Fun Fact: This website was a runner-up to be featured by Swiss Miss. Pretty cool, huh?
